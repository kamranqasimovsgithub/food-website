Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We offer delivery from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please check our menu for details or contact us for specific requests.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS to monitor its status in real-time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery.

Can I schedule an order in advance?

Yes, you can schedule orders up to 7 days in advance through our website or mobile app.
